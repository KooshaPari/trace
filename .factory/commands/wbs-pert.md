---
description: Rapid WBS and PERT expansion for a feature or phase
argument-hint: <feature-or-initiative>
---

Turn `$ARGUMENTS` into a focused delivery plan:

- Capture goals, success metrics, and guardrails from `ARCHITECTURE_BLUEPRINT.md` / `DELIVERY_BLUEPRINT.md`.
- Break work into 4–8 hour WBS tasks with repo paths and validation steps.
- Sketch a PERT diagram (milestones, dependencies, critical path checkpoints).
- Recommend parallel delegations (`plan-orchestrator`, `test-strategist`, `/doc-sync`) and readiness evidence.

Format:

Summary: <headline>
WBS:
- <workstream>: <tasks + estimates + proof>
PERT:
- <milestone>: <depends on + risk>
Delegations:
- <action>: <droid/command + when to trigger>
