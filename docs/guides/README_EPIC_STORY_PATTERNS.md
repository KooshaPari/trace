## Key Takeaways

### 10 Critical Patterns

1. **SAFe Epic Hypothesis** - Testable hypothesis + success metrics before investment
2. **Lean Business Case** - Cost/benefit analysis with MVP definition
3. **Story Mapping** - Backbone (workflow) + ribs (stories) + walking skeleton
4. **INVEST Criteria** - 6 quality attributes; target ≥24/30 score
5. **Given-When-Then AC** - BDD format for testable acceptance criteria
6. **SPIDR Splitting** - 5 systematic ways to decompose stories
7. **Fibonacci Estimation** - Story points (1,2,3,5,8,13,21) with planning poker
8. **Definition of Done** - Explicit quality criteria; automated verification
9. **Dependency Mapping** - Identify and manage story dependencies; minimize chains
10. **WSJF Prioritization** - (Business Value + Time Criticality + Risk Reduction) / Job Size

### 5 Implementation Phases

1. **Phase 1** (Weeks 1-2): Foundation - INVEST, DoD
2. **Phase 2** (Weeks 3-4): Estimation - Fibonacci, velocity
3. **Phase 3** (Weeks 5-6): Advanced - SPIDR, AC patterns
4. **Phase 4** (Weeks 7-8): Enterprise - WSJF, epics
5. **Phase 5** (Weeks 9-10): Automation - CI/CD, dashboards

### Success Metrics
